Larbi Ben M'hidi {1923-04/03/1957}= algerian prominent figure and founding member of FLN, born in Ain M'lila, executed by french forces in Algiers.
Abane Ramdane {1920-27/12/1957}= key architect of the Algerian revolution and organizer of the Soummam Congress, killed by his own comrades in Morocco.
Houari Boumediene {1932-27/12/1978}= second president of Algeria, led the 1965 coup and nationalized oil industry, born in Guelma.
Ahmed Ben Bella {1916-11/04/2012}= first president of independent Algeria and co-founder of FLN, born in Maghnia.
Ferhat Abbas {1899-24/12/1985}= prominent algerian nationalist politician and first president of the GPRA, born in Taher.
Mostefa Ben Boulaïd {1917-22/03/1956}= one of the nine historic leaders of the FLN revolution, born in Arris, killed by a booby-trapped radio.
Krim Belkacem {1922-18/10/1970}= historic leader of the Algerian revolution and key negotiator at Evian, born in Dra El Mizan, assassinated in Frankfurt.
Didouche Mourad {1927-18/01/1955}= one of the historic nine leaders of the FLN, born in Algiers, killed in battle in Constantine region.
Navarino battle {20/10/1827}: a battle between ottoman empire allied forces and the combined British French and Russian fleets resulting in ottoman defeat and greek independence.
Battle of Isly {14/08/1844}: a battle between french forces and moroccan army near Oujda resulting in french victory and weakening of moroccan support for algerian resistance.
Sétif massacre {08/05/1945}: french colonial forces massacred thousands of algerian civilians in Sétif Guelma and Kherrata following VE Day protests.
Proclamation of November 1st {01/11/1954}: the FLN launched the armed independence war against french colonialism beginning the algerian revolution.
Soummam Congress {20/08/1956}: a landmark meeting of FLN leaders in Kabylie that structured the revolution and established political primacy over military.
Evian Accords {18/03/1962}: peace agreements signed between france and the GPRA ending the algerian war of independence after 132 years of colonialism.
Independence of Algeria {05/07/1962}: Algeria officially declared independence from France after a referendum showed overwhelming support for self-determination.
Battle of Algiers {1956-1957}: urban guerrilla warfare campaign by FLN in Algiers casbah brutally suppressed by french paratroopers under General Massu.
